# Olist-Store-Analysis
Project Title
Olist Store Performance: End-to-End Sales, Payments, and Logistics Overview

Short Description
The Olist Store Analysis Dashboard is a comprehensive Power BI report designed to transform raw Brazilian e-commerce data into actionable business intelligence. It provides a "single pane of glass" view into 16.01M in total sales, exploring the intersection of payment behavior, delivery performance, and customer satisfaction across various product categories and regions.

Tech Stack
Power BI Desktop: Core platform for data visualization and report design.

Power Query: Data cleaning and transformation, specifically handling delivery date inconsistencies and merging relational tables.

DAX (Data Analysis Expressions): Utilized for complex measures including Total Sales, Unique Customers, and Average Delivery Lead Times.

Data Modeling: Implementation of a Star Schema to efficiently connect orders, customers, payments, and product dimensions.

Features / Highlights
Business Problem
Managing a multi-vendor marketplace requires identifying operational bottlenecks. Specifically, the need to understand how delivery delays impact review scores and which payment methods are most reliable for high-value transactions.

Goal of the Dashboard
Monitor Growth: Track revenue and order volume trends from 2016 to 2018.

Optimize Logistics: Identify which product categories suffer from the longest delivery delays.

Customer Insights: Analyze payment preferences (Credit Card vs. Boleto) for top-rated orders.

Key Visuals Walkthrough
Executive KPI Cards: Instant visibility into 16.01M Total Sales, 96K Unique Customers, and 3.26M Total Profit.

Sales by Weekend/Weekday: Breakdown showing that 77.21% of transactions occur on weekdays.

Logistics Leaderboard: Analysis of average delivery days by category, identifying "Audio" and "DVDs/Blu-Ray" as high-lag areas (12.5+ days).

Review Score Analysis: A dual-axis chart comparing Average Review Score (4.09) against Average Delivery Days (12.09).

Payment Analysis: Comparison of payment types for 5-star reviews, showing Credit Card as the dominant choice with 44K orders.

Business Impact & Insights
Operational Efficiency: Revealed a correlation between shipping speeds and customer satisfaction, pinpointing "Audio" equipment for logistical optimization.

Strategic Planning: The time-series analysis shows significant scaling beginning in late 2017, providing a baseline for future forecasting.

Regional Performance: Identifies Sao Paulo as the highest-performing city by both payment value and price.
